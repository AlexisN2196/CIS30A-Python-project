
HOW IT WORKS:

    - We'll drop off empty laundry bags for you to fill
    - Let us know you're ready for pick up
    - We'll pick up your laundry and return it to you the next day

FOR LAUNDRY SERVICES:   
   
    * each bag holds about 5 lbs of clothing 
        * green bags are hang dry
        * red bags are machine dry
   

FOR DRY CLEANING:

    * items will be returned hanging in garment bags
